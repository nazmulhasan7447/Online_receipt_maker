    • Key things you need to know about Receipt



    What deos receipt mean?

    A receipt is an acknowledgement provided by the vendor to the buyer. It contains the details of goods or services that are delivered by the seller. Many suppliers and sellers use this receipt as proof for processing returns or refunds or based on their requirements as proof.

    Types of receipt

    There are several types of receipt like Cash Receipt, Expense Receipt, Sales Receipt, Official Receipt, Delivery Receipt etc. These receipts used for acknowledgment of the receiving of the cash amount, proof for the goods or products sold, record the sales, proof of the shipment of ordered goods.
